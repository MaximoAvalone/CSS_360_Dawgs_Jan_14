# Search bar
## User tasks: 
Searching up an artist/song title, browsing artists/song titles, selecting an artist or directly playing a song title, if artist was selected then selecting a song from that artist
## User story: 
Bob Stone is a college student looking for a Kendrick Lamar song to listen to. He clicks on the search bar, enters the text “Kendrick Lamar” and clicks enter. He browses the options sorted by the most popular by name then clicks on Kendrick Lamar. He browses through all Kendrick Lamar songs sorted by release date and selects a song to listen to.

# Queue songs
## User task: 
right click on desired song 
Select add to queue from the dropdown menu
## User Story:
Bob Stone next decides he wants to add songs to the queue so he doesn’t have to manually click play on each song. After finding the song that he wants to play he moves his mouse to hover over the song and right clicks on it which opens the dropdown menu, from this menu he selects the “Queue” button and clicks it. Then he notices that the queue list now displays the new song as a part of the queue.
